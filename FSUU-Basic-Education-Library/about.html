Who We Are

The Morelos Library serves as the learning hub for students, teachers, and the community. We are dedicated to providing quality resources, modern facilities, and a welcoming space for research, reading, and collaboration.

Our Mission

To support the academic growth of the Basic Education Department by offering accessible, up-to-date resources and fostering a love for reading, learning, and discovery.

Our Vision

To be a leading school library that empowers students with knowledge, critical thinking skills, and a passion for lifelong learning.
